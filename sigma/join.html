We are always looking for excellent students to join our group. If this is you, please feel free to drop us a line and indicate how your research interests or experiences match our research. I try my best to respond to emails that are relevant.


Main requirements: (1) Self-motivated. You will not be able to learn much here if you do not motivate yourself or cannot devote much time; (2) Strong mathematical background. We are interested in developing principled and rigorous techniques; (3) Good programming skills (well, self-evident).


Prospective Students:

-- PhD Applicants: We always look for excellent PhD candidates. If you are interested, please apply to the UChicago CS PhD program and indicates your interest in working with me. If you feel it might be helpful to drop me a line, please feel free to do so and I will try my best to respond.

-- Undergrads and master students at UChicago: our group usually admits students who (1) have taken my course or other upper-level math/stats courses, and performed well; and (2) could devote enough time on research. Please highlight these in your email including a time plan.

-- Visitors & Summer Interns: we also actively accept visitors or interns. It will be helpful if (1) you already have some relevant research experience or have recommendations from researchers whom I know; (2) can visit (virtually or physically) for at least two months or more. Please indicate the time range in your email.
