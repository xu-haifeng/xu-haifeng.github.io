We are always looking for excellent students to join our group. If you are interested, please feel free to drop us a line and indicate how your research interests or experiences match our research. I usually try my best to respond to emails that are truly relevant.


Main requirements: (1) Self-motivated. You will not be able to learn much here if you do not motivate yourself or cannot devote much time; (2) Strong mathematical background. We are interested in developing principled and rigorous techniques; (3) Good programming skills (well, self-evident).


Prospective Students:

-- PhD Applicants: We always look for excellent PhD candidates. If you are interested, please apply to UVA CS PhD program from here and indicates your interest in working with me in the application system. If you feel it might be helpful to drop me a line, please feel free to do so and I will try my best to respond.

-- MS and Undergrads at UVA: I only admit students who (1) have taken my course or other math/stats courses, and performed well; and (2) could devote enough time on research. Please highlight these in your email including a time plan.

-- Visitors & Summer Interns: I occasionally accept visitors or interns if (1) there is really a good match or recommendation from researchers whom I know; (2) can visit for at least two months or more. Please indicate the time range in your email.
